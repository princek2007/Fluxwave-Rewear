About the Avengers

ReWear is a community-driven clothing exchange platform that promotes sustainable fashion by encouraging people to swap, share, and reuse wearable garments instead of throwing them away. Our mission is to reduce textile waste, empower conscious consumers, and build a circular fashion system that benefits both the planet and the people. Together, we can make second-hand the first choice.
